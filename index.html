Biography

Xian Liu is a first-year Ph.D. student at Multi-Media Laboratory (MMLab), Department of Information Engineering in The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK). He is supervised by Prof. Dahua Lin, Prof. Ziwei Liu and Prof. Bolei Zhou. He received the Bachelor's degree from College of Computer Science and Technology at Zhejiang University in 2021, supervised by Prof. Xiaowei Zhou. His research interests include generative modeling and audio-visual learning. He is very fortunate to collaborate closely with Dr. Hang Zhou, Dr. Ceyuan Yang, Yinghao Xu, Qianyi Wu, Yuanqi Du and Rui Qian.
I am always open to discussions and collaborations, feel free to drop me an email if you are interested in :)
