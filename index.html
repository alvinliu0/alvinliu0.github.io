About me

I am currently a fourth-year Ph.D. student in Multimedia Lab (MMLab), the Chinese University of Hong Kong. I am supervised by Prof. Xiaogang Wang and Prof. Ziwei Liu. I have closely collaborated with Xudong Xu, Dr. Yu Liu and Prof. Ping Luo. I received my bachelor's degree in Acoustics from the School of Physics at Nanjing University (NJU) in 2017.

My research interests include deep learning and its applications on audio-visual learning and face generation.
